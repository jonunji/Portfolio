Welcome to my portfolio! Below are a few of the projects I created.

Mousebound

Play in a 3D world, using a 2D system.
Move your character by placing your mouse cursor in the direction you want to go.
Made independently using Unreal Engine 4 and C++.

Linear Release

A puzzle game where you use a bow and arrow to solve puzzles
The arrow has the ability to interact, push, ricochet, teleport, and transfer electricity.
Collaboratively made using Unity and C#.

About

Hi, I'm Jonathan (some people call me Jonji, sounds like Jumanji)! I am studying Computer Science at the University of Central Florida, expecting to graduate in Spring 2023. I find programming lots of fun, especially when I can be creative with it, which is why I have a lot of time invested in game development!

Another reason I have a lot of time invested: If you have ever programmed before, you know the magical feeling of getting really complex logic to finally work out. You also know what it's like to try to solve a problem for 4 hours when you could have been done with it within 20 minutes normally. Sometimes, making the perfect program is what's more important.

When I'm not trying to get Linux to run correctly, you can find me at the gym (reluctantly), playing new songs on guitar, cooking some more schnitzel, playing video games with friends, or watching a new show.

If you'd like to reach out to me for business, or maybe you just want to talk about Better Call Saul, do so here. I'd be delighted to hear from you!
